Pistachio Pudding

Pistachio pudding that's great for the whole family!

This extra-healthy pistachio pudding is made with real pistachios to give it some nutty crunch. The color comes from fresh spinach. Using spinach is a much safer alternative to artificial food coloring, and it adds a nutritional boost.

Always a hit, this pistachio pudding will sure liven up your next get together and have everyone talking about it for days to come. In under 15 minutes, you can have this healthy dessert ready to be eaten.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Pistachios
  • 15 ounce Coconut Milk
  • 1/2 cup Spinach
  • 2 tablespoons Tapioca Flour
  • 1/4 cup Maple Syrup
  • 1 teaspoon Almond Extract
  • Dark or Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips
  • Vanilla Extract

Optional Ingredients

  • Whole Pistachios as Garnish
  • Whipped Cream as Garnish

Steps

  1. Grind the pistachios in a food processor or blender to make fine crumbs, then set aside.
  2. Blend the coconut milk and spinach until smooth.
  3. Combine the tapioca flour with the milk mixture in a saucepan and cook over medium-low heat for about 8 minutes, until thickened.
  4. Remove the thickened spinach milk from the heat and stir in the ground pistachios, maple syrup, and almond extract.
  5. Pour the mixture into pudding cups, cover, and refrigerate for 3 hours until set.
  6. Use whipped cream and/or additional pistachios as a garnish, if desired, and serve.
