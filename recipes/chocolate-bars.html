No-Bake Chocolate Bars

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Honey
  • Coconut Oil
  • Rolled Oats
  • Shredded Coconut
  • Chopped Pecans
  • Dark or Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips
  • Vanilla Extract

Preparations

  1. Melt the peanut butter, honey, and coconut oil in a medium-size saucepan over medium-low heat. Stir frequently as the mixture melts.
  2. When it has melted, remove from the heat and add the chocolate. Stir until the chocolate is completely smooth.
  3. Add the oats, coconut, and pecans. Stir to combine. Pour the mixture into an 8×8 pan. Refrigerate until the chocolate hardens, at least 3-4 hours.
  4. Slice into 1"-2" square pieces. Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator.
